Fright Night Flip

Objective

The goal of the game is to find all the matching pairs of cards with the fewest possible moves.

How to Play

  • Click on a card to flip it over and reveal the image on the front.
  • Click on a second card to flip it over.
  • If the images on the two flipped cards match, they will remain face up.
  • If the images do not match, the cards will flip back over after a short delay.
  • Repeat the process to find all the matching pairs.

Moves

The number of wrong moves you make is tracked and displayed on the screen. A move is counted each time you flip two unmatched cards.

Winning the Game

The game is won when all pairs of cards have been matched and are face up. A message will appear congratulating you on your win.

Game Over

If you reach the maximum number of allowed wrong flips (6 incorrect pairs), the game will display a "Game Over" message. You will not be able to flip any more cards until the game is reset.

Resetting the Game

To start a new game, click the "Reset" button. The cards will be reshuffled, and the game board will be reset. All cards will be revealed for a few seconds at the beginning of the new game, then they will flip back over, and you can start finding matches again.

Good luck and have fun playing Fright Night Flip!
